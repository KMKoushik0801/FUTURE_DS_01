## Project Overview
This project is part of my task-based internship at **Future Interns**, where I analyzed the Superstore dataset to extract actionable business insights and built an interactive dashboard for data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze sales, profit, and quantity trends over multiple years
- Identify top-performing products and high-profit sub-categories
- Compare performance across major categories:
  - Technology
  - Furniture
  - Office Supplies
- Present insights using dashboards, KPIs, and visualizations

---

## Key Insights
- Technology category contributed the highest overall profit
- Certain sub-categories consistently outperformed others in terms of revenue
- Seasonal trends were observed in sales and profit across years
- A small number of products generated a large share of total profit
